Privacy Policy for Zen Soundscape

Last updated: January 2026

Zen Soundscape (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our mobile app.

Zen Soundscape is designed to be a privacy-first app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app uses only local audio files for ambient sounds. Saved mixes remain on your device and are never uploaded.

3. Permissions Used

4. Third-Party Services

Zen Soundscape does not use third-party ads, analytics, or tracking services.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed.
